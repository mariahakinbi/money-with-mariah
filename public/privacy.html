Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Money with Mariah ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website moneywithmariah.com. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide, such as your email address when you subscribe to our newsletter or book a coaching session. We also collect standard analytics data (pages visited, time on site, device type) through Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site.

How We Use Your Information

We use your email address to send you newsletter content and updates about our services. We use analytics data to improve our website and understand which content is most helpful. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data to help us understand site traffic. Kit (ConvertKit) manages our email newsletter and stores subscriber email addresses. Calendly handles session booking and may collect your name and email. Stripe processes payments securely and collects payment information directly. Amazon Associates and Walmart Affiliates track purchases made through our affiliate links using cookies.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies for analytics tracking and affiliate link attribution. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some site features may not function as intended.

Affiliate Links

Some links on our site are affiliate links to Amazon and Walmart. When you click these links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. These affiliate programs use cookies to track referrals.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Your Rights

You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email. You can request deletion of your personal data by contacting us directly. If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the CCPA.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website or social media channels.
